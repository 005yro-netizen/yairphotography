Our Complete Work Process - From First Call to Finished Album

Step 1: Initial Consultation and Planning ??

Every event begins with a personal conversation with you. We'll discuss your expectations, the atmosphere you want to create, and the important moments you don't want to miss. Together, we'll choose the photography package that fits your event and budget perfectly.

Step 2: Event Day - Arrival and Preparation ??

Early Arrival (20 Minutes Before)

I arrive at the event 20 minutes before the official start time so I can get to know the space, check the lighting, identify the best angles, and make sure the equipment is ready. This keeps surprises at bay and ensures I'm ready the moment the first guests arrive.

Step 3: Family Photos - The First and Most Important Moment ???????????

Why Start with Family?

  • Everyone still has full energy � not tired, not frustrated, simply excited.
  • Clothes are still perfect � the kids haven�t gotten dirty yet and dresses and suits look fresh.
  • The atmosphere is positive � everyone is looking forward to the celebration and it shows in the photos.

I guide everyone naturally and comfortably, making sure everyone looks great, and capture the most beautiful moments, both posed and spontaneous.

Step 4: Reception - The First Guests ??

  • The first hugs.
  • Warm congratulations.
  • The hosts' excitement.
  • The emerging atmosphere.

I photograph without interrupting, staying present but not prominent so guests feel free and natural.

Step 5: Event Begins - Sitting Down to Dine ???

  • The vibe around each table.
  • The conversations and laughter.
  • The happy faces.
  • The connections between people.
  • The small details that complete the story.

Each table is its own little world, and I make sure to capture the uniqueness of every group.

Step 6: Dancing - Peak Energy ????

  • The pure joy that bursts from the dancers.
  • The wide smiles.
  • The spontaneous moments that can�t be planned.
  • The energy that spreads to everyone.

I try not to miss a single moment. My eyes are on the dance floor, on the faces, on the magical moments that happen when nobody expects them. I know when to get close, when to shoot from afar, and how to capture the real joy.

Step 7: Between Dances - Continued Documentation ??

  • New guests arriving to congratulate the parents or couple.
  • Intimate moments of meaningful conversations.
  • Connections between generations � grandparents with grandchildren.
  • The little details that tell the story.

I'm always looking for the story � not just the pretty picture, but the real moment that will remind you how you felt.

Step 8: End of Event - Final Family Photo ??

  • The extended family (everyone still at the venue).
  • Small groups who want a photo together.
  • The last moments before saying goodbye.

This is the moment to capture everyone together again, this time with the energy and joy of a successful celebration.

Step 9: Goodbye and Beginning Technical Work ??

Immediate Backup

The moment I get home, the first thing I do is double backup all the photos. Two copies in different locations ensure no image gets lost.

Professional Editing

I import every photo into professional editing software and go through them individually:

  • Correcting lighting and colors.
  • Sharpening and improving quality.
  • Creating a cohesive atmosphere throughout the album.
  • Removing small imperfections.

The result: Professional-level photos that look exactly like you remember the moment � but even more beautiful.

Step 10: Receiving Photos - Within 4 Days ??

Within 4 days of the event�s conclusion you receive a private link to all the photos:

  • An organized, easy-to-view gallery.
  • Simple download options.
  • The ability to share with family and friends.

You don�t have to wait weeks � I know you want to see the photos quickly and make sure that happens as soon as possible.

Step 11: Selecting Photos for the Album ??

  • You go through the gallery at your own pace.
  • You choose the photos you love most.
  • You send me your selection.

No pressure, no deadlines. This is your album and you should feel great about every photo in it.

Step 12: Album Design - Full Collaboration ??

After you select the images, I pass them to the professional graphic designer I work with.

Transparent and Guided Design Process

  • The designer reaches out to you directly.
  • She presents the initial layout.
  • She listens to your wishes and adjustments.
  • She makes changes until you are 100% satisfied.

Only after your final approval does the album go to print. No album is printed without you saying �yes, this is exactly what we wanted.�

Step 13: Printing and Delivery ??

  • Sent to a high-quality print house I have worked with for years.
  • Printed with the finest standards.
  • Bound in a durable, premium cover.
  • Carefully packaged and delivered straight to your door.

Step 14: The Album in Your Hands ??

The moment you open the album for the first time is the moment I worked for. I hope you love it, revisit it again and again, and that these photos remind you not only how it looked but how it felt.

Thank you for choosing me to be part of your special day. It was an honor to capture the moments that will stay with you forever.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your event.

  • How much does event photography cost?

    Prices vary depending on the type of event, duration, and photography package you choose. All details and exact prices are available on our website at YairRozenberg.com. I recommend reviewing the different packages and then we can talk by phone to find the package that best suits you.

  • What's included in the photography package?

    • Professional photography throughout the entire event
    • Early arrival 20 minutes before the event
    • Family photography at the beginning of the event
    • Full documentation of all stages (reception, meal, dancing)
    • Professional editing of all photos
    • Organized digital gallery
    • Delivery of all photos within 4 days

    More advanced packages also include a printed album, video coverage, and additional options.

  • Do you offer discounts for multiple events or early booking?

    Absolutely! If you're booking more than one event (for example, a wedding + family bar mitzvah), or booking several months in advance, there's an option for a discount. Contact me and we'll find a price that works for you.

  • How do I book you for an event?

    1. Call me or send a message through the website
    2. We'll talk about your event and expectations
    3. Together we'll choose the appropriate photography package
    4. We'll sign an agreement and you'll pay a deposit to secure the date
    5. That's it - the date is reserved for you!

  • How far in advance should I book?

    The earlier the better. Events during peak season (spring-fall) tend to fill up quickly. I recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance, but if it's urgent - it's always worth checking my availability. Sometimes you get lucky!

  • What happens if I need to change the event date?

    I understand that sometimes things change. If you need to change the date, I'll try as much as possible to accommodate myself to the new date. It's important to update me as early as possible so we can find a solution.

  • What camera do you use?

    I use one of the best professional cameras on the Israeli market. The camera excels in all lighting conditions and enables sharp, detailed close-up shots, strong zoom without quality loss, portraits with cinematic depth of field, wide landscape shots, and fast capture of spontaneous moments.

    Additionally, I always arrive with backup equipment - in case something goes wrong (which almost never happens).

  • Do you come alone or with an assistant?

    I come alone to most events, but if the event is particularly large or if you want video coverage - I can bring a professional videographer I work with. This ensures all angles are covered and no moment is missed.

  • How many photos will I receive?

    It depends on the event duration and what happens during it, but on average:

    • 3-4 hour event: between 300-500 edited photos
    • Larger event: can reach 700-800 photos

    Important to note - I don't send blurry or failed photos. You only receive the best photos, edited and ready.

  • How do you know which moments to photograph?

    That's an excellent question! After 7 years and over 300 events, I know how to recognize when something is about to happen:

    • The look between mother and daughter before they embrace
    • The moment before the dance explodes
    • The spontaneous laughter that bursts out
    • The tear falling during a moving moment

    Additionally, before the event we talk about the moments that are especially important to you, and I make sure not to miss any of them.

  • What time do you arrive?

    I arrive 20 minutes before the official event start. For example, if the event starts at 6:00 PM, I'm already there at 5:40 PM. This gives me time to familiarize myself with the venue, check lighting, and ensure everything is ready.

  • Until what time do you stay?

    I stay until the end of the event - including the final family photo. If the event runs longer than planned, I don't leave in the middle. I'm here until the last guest leaves.

  • When will I receive the photos?

    Within 4 days of the event's conclusion, you receive a link to a full gallery with all the edited photos. This is relatively fast for the industry, because I know you're dying to see the photos!

  • How long does it take to receive the printed album?

    After you've selected the photos and approved the design with the designer, the printing and binding process usually takes 2-3 weeks. I do everything to expedite the process, but I don't compromise on quality.

  • What does your editing style look like?

    I prefer a natural and authentic editing style - not too fancy or artificial. The photos look like the real moment, just at its best:

    • Warm, pleasant colors
    • Balanced lighting
    • Professional sharpening
    • Without exaggerated filters

    I want that in 10 years you'll look at the photos and say "wow, what a day that was", not "wow, what weird editing".

  • Can I request changes to photos?

    Absolutely. If there's a specific photo you want to improve or change something in - tell me and I'll do it. I want you to be 100% satisfied.

  • Do you do retouching on photos?

    Yes, I do basic retouching on all photos (lighting correction, colors, minor cleanup). If more advanced retouching is needed (removing skin blemishes, teeth whitening, etc.) - it's usually for an additional fee, but it depends on the scope. We'll discuss this in advance.

  • Why do you start with family photography?

    This is one of the most important decisions! I start with family photography because:

    • Everyone still has energy - not tired from the event
    • Clothes are perfect - kids haven't gotten dirty yet
    • The atmosphere is good - everyone is in good anticipation and happy
    • There's time - we can organize everyone without pressure

    Family photos taken at the end of the event often look tired and less good.

  • Can you photograph specific family configurations?

    Absolutely! Tell me in advance which configurations you want (for example: entire family, just parents with children, second generation, all grandchildren with grandparents, etc.) and I'll make sure to capture all these photos.

  • Do you also film video?

    I specialize in still photography (photos), but I work with a professional videographer who can come to the event. If you want video as well - just tell me and I'll handle all the coordination.

  • How much does it cost to add video coverage?

    It depends on the video length and type of editing, but usually it's an addition of about 30-40% to the photography package price. We'll talk about it in our conversation and find something that fits your budget.

  • What happens if the camera breaks in the middle of the event?

    I always arrive with a backup camera. If something happens (which is really rare), I switch to the second camera within seconds. No moment is lost.

  • How do you back up the photos?

    The moment I get home, I do a double backup:

    • First backup on external hard drive
    • Second backup in the cloud

    This means there are two copies in different locations, and there's no chance anything gets lost.

  • What's your guarantee?

    I offer a 100% full money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the photos. Period. I'm confident in what I do, and I want you to be completely at ease.

  • Where are you located?

    I'm based in Jerusalem, but I photograph events throughout the country - from north to south.

  • Is there an additional charge for travel?

    It depends on the distance:

    • Events in Jerusalem and surroundings - no addition
    • Events in central Israel - minimal addition
    • Events in the north/south - there's an addition for fuel and travel

    We'll discuss this in our conversation and clarify everything in advance, without surprises.

  • Do you also photograph abroad?

    If it's a special event and you're willing to cover flight and accommodation costs - absolutely! Talk to me and we'll see how we can arrange it.

  • Is there a contract?

    Yes, there's a simple and clear contract that defines date and time, price and package, inclusions/exclusions, rights, and cancellation terms. Everything is transparent, without fine print.

  • Who owns the photos?

    You receive full usage rights to the photos for personal and family use. You can print, share, upload to social media - whatever you want. The copyright remains with me as the photographer, which means I may use some of the photos for my portfolio or marketing. If you don't want certain photos published - just tell me.

  • What happens if I need to cancel the event?

    Cancellation terms depend on notice:

    • More than 60 days before - full refund of deposit
    • 30-60 days before - 50% refund
    • Less than 30 days - no refund

    But if it's a real emergency, I'm always willing to talk and find a fair solution.

  • Is it possible to print large photos for framing?

    Yes! I can arrange quality printing of large photos for the wall. This is done at a professional printing house I work with, and you receive the photo printed on quality paper or canvas.

  • Can I buy additional albums?

    Absolutely. If you want an additional album for parents, grandparents, or another copy for yourself - I'll arrange it. The price is cheaper than the first album because the design already exists.

  • How can I contact you?

    The easiest way is to call or send a WhatsApp message. I usually respond within a few hours, and I'm always happy to answer additional questions.

    All contact details are available on the website: YairRozenberg.com
