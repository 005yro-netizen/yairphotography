About

I'm Yair Rozenberg, a photographer based in Jerusalem. I document people and stories — from family milestones to quiet, honest portraits.

My approach is calm and attentive: I guide when needed, and step back when moments unfold by themselves.

If you're planning an event or a session and want natural, meaningful images, I'd be happy to talk.
